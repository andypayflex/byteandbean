About Us

Work Your Way

Choose from dedicated desks, lounge seating, or window bars with views of the Muizenberg mountains.

Byte & Bean is where Muizenberg's remote workers, freelancers, and digital nomads come together. We've created a space that combines the productivity of a professional workspace with the comfort and community of your favourite coffee shop.

Whether you need a quiet corner to focus, a meeting table for collaborations, or just a cosy couch to brainstorm, we've got you covered. And yes, your furry friends are welcome too.

  • Dedicated hot desks with monitors
  • Relaxed lounge areas for casual work
  • High-speed fibre WiFi
  • Fresh food & artisan coffee all day
